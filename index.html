Home

The purpose of Youth for Human Rights International is to teach youth about human rights, specifically the United Nations Universal Declaration of Human Rights, and inspire them to become valuable advocates for tolerance and peace.

Children are the future of this world. Education programs that teach them their human rights — and how to defend and protect them — forward the message of tolerance and cooperation, and the ultimate goal of universal human rights.

Learn what your human rights are and help make them known to others.
